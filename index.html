    About Us

    COMEST, the computer science society of Dyal Singh College(M), is all set to take you on a fun-filled annual tech fest. Since past 10 years, COMEST has been organising various seminars, workshops, excursions with various IT personalities. It aims at introducing and promoting talent in coding and gaming with a pinch of frolic. COMEST brings an opportunity to awaken your skills and also win some exciting prizes by simply engaging you in activities you might be good at.

    Counter Strike

    Wanna fight??Let's see how much strength you have..Squad Counter Strike challange.

    Despription:-

    Bring your team to FragByte, the LAN battle of Counter Strike where only skills and tactical strategies win. Whether the strategy is to hold mid or rush B, where enemy might be holding any crucial angle work as a team to defeat the opposing force and claim your victory.

    1. You may bring the following peripherals only  Mouse  Keyboard  Mouse pad  Headphones
    (Bring at your on risk)
    2. Scripts are not allowed
    3. Auto-sniper is banned for all teams
    4. The matches will be 5 v 5 competitive matches of 20 rounds
    a) Each round will be 1 minute long.
    b) Teams will switch sides after 10 rounds.
    c) In case of a tie, tie breaker rounds will be held
    In case of any technical difficulty alert the admin immediately
    Failure to comply with the rules will result in immediate disqualification.
    Any unfair means like hacking or scripting will lead to immediate disqualification
    In case any issue arises admin/organiser’s decision shall be final.

    Team Size:-5 members each team
    Date:-2 March,2020
    Venue:- Lab 1003
    Timings:- 2:00 PM Onwards
